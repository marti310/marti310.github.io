Website Workshop Pre-work

Prior to the website workshop, complete the following steps. All steps should take no more than 30 minutes. The pre-work will need to be completed so that you can draft your website during the workshop.
  • Go on the internet and find 5-8 mathematicians such as professors, graduate students, postdocs or undergraduates that have websites. Save these websites links.
  • Pick your top 3-5 favorite websites. Save these websites links.
  • Check which platform these mathematicians are using to host their websites. Some could be on Google Sites, GitHub, Wix, etc.
  • Check for common themes among these websites such as format, content, etc.
  • Pick your favorite platform where you want to host your website.
  • Create an account on the platform you will create your website: Google sites, GitHub or Wix - all require an account.
  • Come ready to work on your website!
